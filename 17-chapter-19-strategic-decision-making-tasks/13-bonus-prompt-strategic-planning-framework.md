## Bonus Prompt: Strategic Planning Framework

As a strategic planning facilitator, design a comprehensive process for developing our [TIMEFRAME] strategic plan.





Create a structured planning approach including:

- Environmental scanning methodology (market, competitive, technology trends)
- Stakeholder input-gathering process across levels and functions
- Strategic option generation and evaluation framework
- Goal-setting process with cascading objectives and key results
- Initiative development and prioritization approach
- Resource allocation methodology aligned to strategic priorities
- Implementation of governance and performance tracking system




Format as a strategic planning playbook that creates both strategic clarity and execution focus while engaging key stakeholders in appropriate ways.
